About

IPAR Consult Ltd is a registered research and management consulting arm of the Institute of Policy Analysis and Research(IPAR-Rwanda), the leading think-tank in Rwanda. IPAR Consult Ltd offers innovative research, impact evaluation, management consulting and capacity building services to the public sector, development partners, local and international non-profit organizations and the private sector.

Vision

To be a leading international consulting firm in research and management services.

Mission

We are committed to clients’ satisfaction through improved performance by providing customized, timely and quality research and management consulting services in Rwanda and beyond. Core Values

IPAR Consult Ltd is guided by the following values :

  • Integrity – engage in utmost honesty ;
  • Excellence – deliver quality with professionalism ;
  • Innovativeness – creativity in delivering solutions ;
  • Client focus – delivering to the client’s expectations;
